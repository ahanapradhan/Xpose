"The business analysis focuses on identifying and summarizing online sales orders that were shipped from multiple warehouses and subsequently returned, within a specific 60-day period starting from April 1, 2000. The analysis is limited to orders shipped to customers in Indiana and associated with a specific company named 'pri'. The query calculates the total number of distinct orders, the total shipping costs, and the total net profit for these transactions. The results are sorted by the number of distinct orders and limited to the top 100 entries, providing insights into the logistics and financial performance of these specific sales and returns."
