"The business analysis focuses on identifying and summing up the sales revenue generated in June 1998 from catalog and web sales channels. The analysis specifically targets items that were frequently sold in-store over a four-year period starting from 1998 and customers who are among the top spenders, contributing more than 95% of the maximum store sales. The query aims to provide insights into the performance of popular products and high-value customers across different sales channels, helping the business understand key revenue drivers during that period."
