"The business operation involves retrieving detailed information about suppliers and parts from a database, specifically focusing on parts of size 15 and type containing 'BRASS' that are supplied by suppliers located in European nations. The query aims to identify suppliers offering these parts at the lowest supply cost within Europe. The results are organized by descending account balance of the suppliers, followed by the nation name, supplier name, and part key, providing a prioritized list of suppliers and parts that meet the specified criteria."
