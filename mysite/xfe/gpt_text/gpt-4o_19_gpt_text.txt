"This SQL query calculates the total revenue generated from the sale of specific products that meet certain criteria. It focuses on products from three specific brands ('Brand#12', 'Brand#23', and 'Brand#34') and considers only those items that are shipped by air and delivered in person. The products are further filtered based on their container type, quantity, and size. For 'Brand#12', the containers are small, quantities range from 1 to 11, and sizes are between 1 and 5. For 'Brand#23', the containers are medium, quantities range from 10 to 20, and sizes are between 1 and 10. For 'Brand#34', the containers are large, quantities range from 20 to 30, and sizes are between 1 and 15. The revenue is calculated by summing up the extended price of these products after applying the discount."
