"This SQL query calculates the annual profit for each nation by summing up the profit amounts derived from sales of products with names containing the word 'COLOR'. The profit is calculated by taking the extended price of each line item, applying any discounts, and subtracting the supply cost multiplied by the quantity sold. The results are grouped by nation and year, and then ordered by nation and year in descending order, providing a clear view of profitability trends over time for each nation."
